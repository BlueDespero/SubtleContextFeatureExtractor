PSALMS.
Chapter 133.
A song for pilgrims going up to Jerusalem. A psalm of David. How good, how delightful it is when people live together lin harmony! 
This is as precious as the oil used to anoint Aaron, running down from his head onto his beard, onto the collar of his clothes. 
It's like the dew of Mount Hermon that falls on Zion's mountains. That is where the Lord gave his blessing of life that lasts forever.
